Welcome to the barebones launch of my thesis website, wherein I begin to revisit html/css (gulp). For now, I think getting the material links up to my writing is more crucial than making a fancy pretty little site... after all, I kind of miss Web 1.0 when everything was quite boring and easy to parse.
